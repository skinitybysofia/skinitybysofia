Botox – Anti-Aging Treatment

Botox is a non-surgical treatment that uses botulinum toxin type A to temporarily reduce muscle activity, smoothing expression lines and dynamic wrinkles. It is especially effective for forehead wrinkles, between the eyebrows (glabella), and around the eyes (crow's feet).

Benefits of Botox:

  • Smooths fine lines and dynamic wrinkles;
  • Softens forehead lines and expression wrinkles in the glabella;
  • Reduces crow's feet around the eyes;
  • Helps prevent the deepening of wrinkles over time;
  • Provides natural, discreet, and rejuvenated results;
  • Enhances facial harmony, boosting self-esteem and confidence.

This quick and minimally invasive procedure delivers natural and elegant results, helping you regain a fresher, younger appearance without altering your facial expressions. Additionally, Botox requires no downtime, allowing you to quickly return to your daily activities. The effects typically last between 3 to 6 months, making it a safe and effective solution for those seeking subtle and lasting facial rejuvenation.

Quick Recovery

Botox is a fast and virtually painless procedure that requires no recovery time. Some redness or slight swelling at the injection sites is normal and usually disappears within a few hours. Results begin to appear after a few days, reaching full effect within 10 to 14 days, with an average duration of 3 to 4 months.

Second Consultation and Free Top-Up

Client satisfaction is our priority. Therefore, we offer a free follow-up consultation, usually performed about 2 weeks after the initial treatment. This session allows us to assess the results and ensure you are fully satisfied with the Botox effect.

If needed, we provide a free top-up (touch-up) to adjust or improve the results, ensuring a natural and balanced appearance. This personalized follow-up is part of our commitment to offering safe, effective treatments that highlight your best version.

Microneedling

Microneedling with PRP and Exosomes

Microneedling with PRP and Exosomes is a cutting-edge, minimally invasive procedure that rejuvenates your skin from within. Tiny, controlled needles stimulate the skin's natural healing process, while PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) uses your own blood, making it a completely natural, artificial-product-free procedure. Exosomes provide a next-generation regenerative boost, enhancing cellular communication, accelerating tissue repair, and improving overall skin health.

This revolutionary procedure restores glow, firmness, and smoothness, reduces fine lines, scars, and imperfections, and delivers naturally youthful results without surgery. It's a favorite among clients seeking luxury, safe, and effective skin rejuvenation.

It boosts collagen and elastin production, improves hydration, texture, tone, and radiance, leaving the skin smooth, revitalized, and naturally luminous.

Product type

PRP and Exosomes - PRP uses your own blood to stimulate natural skin repair, while Exosomes boost regeneration and collagen for radiant, youthful skin.

Treatment time

45-60 minutes

Downtime

Minimal; mild redness or sensitivity may appear for 24-48 hours

Results

Gradual, natural-looking improvements over 2-4 weeks; optimal results after 2-3 sessions

Longevity

Long-lasting skin rejuvenation with proper skincare

Consultation

Complimentary, tailored to your skin and goals

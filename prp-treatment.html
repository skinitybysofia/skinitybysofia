microneedling – collagen induction & skin rejuvenation

Microneedling is a clinically proven skin rejuvenation treatment that stimulates your body's natural healing process to promote smoother, firmer, and more youthful skin. Using fine, sterile needles to create microchannels in the skin, this treatment triggers collagen production and enhances the absorption of regenerative serums.

Microneedling is versatile and highly effective, with minimal downtime and long-lasting results — suitable for various skin concerns including texture, tone, and scarring.

microneedling with prp (platelet-rich plasma)

Known as the "Vampire Facial," this advanced treatment combines microneedling with your body's own growth factors.

  • How it works: A small amount of your blood is drawn, spun in a centrifuge to isolate the PRP, and then applied or injected into the skin during microneedling.
  • Benefits: Stimulates collagen and elastin, accelerates healing, reduces inflammation, and improves skin tone and texture.
  • Ideal for: Dull skin, fine lines, acne scars, and early signs of ageing.

microneedling with growth factors

These lab-derived bioactive proteins mimic the body's natural healing messengers.

  • How it works: Growth factor serums are applied during the microneedling session to enhance cell turnover and skin regeneration.
  • Benefits: Boosts collagen production, accelerates repair, improves texture, and enhances overall skin quality.
  • Ideal for: Skin showing early signs of ageing, uneven tone, and post-acne marks.

microneedling with exosomes

Exosomes represent the latest innovation in regenerative skincare.

  • How it works: Derived from stem cells, exosomes are tiny vesicles rich in growth factors, peptides, and antioxidants. They supercharge the skin's ability to heal and regenerate when applied during or after microneedling.
  • Benefits: Stimulates intense cellular repair, improves elasticity, reduces pigmentation, and delivers a youthful glow.
  • Ideal for: Ageing, dull, or environmentally damaged skin looking for high-tech renewal.

quick recovery

Microneedling typically involves minimal downtime. Mild redness, tightness, or sensitivity may occur for 24–48 hours post-treatment. Most clients return to normal activities the next day.

indicated for:

  • Fine lines and wrinkles
  • Enlarged pores
  • Acne scars or other textural concerns
  • Hyperpigmentation and sun damage
  • Dull or uneven skin tone
  • Skin laxity and early signs of ageing

Experience rejuvenated, resilient skin through collagen activation and cutting-edge regenerative science. Microneedling offers visible results with a natural, healthy glow — powered by your own biology or advanced bioactive serums.

Microneedling with PRP, Exosomes & Growth Factors

Natural Skin Regeneration and Advanced Hair Stimulation

Microneedling is a minimally invasive treatment that uses fine needles to create microchannels in the skin, triggering the body's natural healing response. This stimulates collagen and elastin production, improving skin texture, firmness, elasticity, and radiance.

When combined with PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma), exosomes, or growth factors, the results are significantly enhanced due to the powerful regenerative properties of these biological agents.

How It Works

Microneedling with PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma)

Also known as the "Vampire Facial," this treatment uses your own blood. After centrifugation, the PRP is extracted and applied or injected into the skin during microneedling. Rich in proteins and growth factors, PRP accelerates healing, reduces inflammation, and promotes deep skin regeneration.

Microneedling with Growth Factors

Lab-derived bioactive proteins that mimic the body's natural repair messengers. Applied during the session, they stimulate cell turnover, boost collagen production, and enhance skin renewal.

Microneedling with Exosomes

One of the most advanced innovations in regenerative aesthetics. Exosomes are tiny vesicles rich in growth factors, peptides, and antioxidants, derived from stem cells. They supercharge the skin's repair capacity, improve elasticity, reduce pigmentation, and deliver a youthful glow.

Indications

This treatment is versatile and can be performed on the face, to:

  • Rejuvenate the skin
  • Reduce fine lines and wrinkles
  • Minimise acne scars
  • Improve texture, firmness, and luminosity
  • Boost hydration and even skin tone

And also on the scalp, to:

  • Stimulate hair growth
  • Strengthen hair follicles
  • Naturally treat hair thinning and shedding

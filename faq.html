Frequently Asked Questions

1. What does the treatment process at Skinity involve?
At Skinity, every step is designed to ensure safe, transparent, and personalised care. Our goal is for you to feel informed, confident, and supported throughout your journey.
Step 1: Free Online Assessment
Your first contact is through a free online assessment with a qualified professional. During this session, we explore your aesthetic concerns, medical history, and goals. Based on this, we recommend the most suitable treatments, clearly explaining their benefits, limitations, and realistic expectations. You will also be guided with pre-treatment care instructions to ensure you arrive fully prepared for your in-person appointment.
Step 2: Informed Consent & Appointment Scheduling
If you decide to proceed, you will be sent an informed consent document detailing the proposed treatment - its purpose, method, possible risks, contraindications, side effects, and aftercare. This document should be carefully reviewed and digitally signed. Only after receiving the signed consent can your in-person appointment be confirmed.
Step 3: In-Person Treatment Session
On the scheduled day, you will be welcomed in a safe, fully compliant clinical environment. A thorough facial assessment will be performed, and any questions or concerns will be fully addressed prior to the treatment. The procedure will be performed with technical precision, focusing on facial harmony, using high-quality medical products, always aiming for subtle, safe, and natural-looking results. At the end, you will receive personalised aftercare instructions to optimise your results and minimise any side effects.
Step 4: Ongoing Support & Follow-Up
After your treatment, you will have direct access to the clinic for any questions or concerns. We may also contact you for a post-treatment review, and if clinically indicated, you can schedule a complimentary follow-up appointment.
2. Are treatments suitable for those who have never had aesthetic procedures before?
Yes. All treatments are customised to your individual needs and expectations. First-time clients receive clear, informed guidance at every stage to ensure safety and confidence.
3. How do I know which treatment is right for me?
During your free online assessment, we assess your concerns, medical history, and aesthetic goals to recommend the safest and most effective treatment options to achieve natural and balanced results.
4. What are the most common treatments at Skinity?
Our most popular procedures include:
• Botulinum toxin (Botox®) for expression lines on the forehead, glabella, and crow's feet
• Skinboosters for deep hydration and improved skin texture
• Facial microneedling to stimulate collagen and even out skin tone
• Dermal fillers to enhance volume, define lips, or soften smile lines
5. How long do the results last?
Duration varies by treatment and individual metabolism, but generally:
• Botox: 3 to 4 months
• Skinboosters: 4 to 6 months
• Microneedling: progressive results visible after 3 to 6 sessions
• Dermal fillers: typically 6 to 12 months, depending on the product and area treated
6. Can treatments be combined in the same session?
Yes, if clinically safe. For example, Botox and skinboosters can be done on the same day, but Botox and microneedling should not be combined. This will be carefully planned based on your needs and safety.
7. Is there any downtime after treatments?
Most procedures require little to no downtime. You may experience slight redness, swelling, or minor bruising at injection sites, which usually resolves within a few days. You will receive specific aftercare instructions.
8. Are the treatments painful?
Discomfort varies, but treatments are generally well tolerated. Very fine needles are used, and local anaesthetic can be applied when necessary to minimise any discomfort.
9. Will Botox results leave me looking "frozen"?
No. Our approach focuses on subtle, balanced results that preserve your natural facial expressions. The goal is to soften lines while maintaining your unique identity. You will still look like yourself - just more refreshed and rested.
10. Are treatments suitable for men?
Absolutely. All treatments are suitable for men and women. Many male clients choose Botox, skinboosters, or microneedling to improve skin texture, reduce wrinkles, and refresh their appearance discreetly and naturally.
11. Is a deposit required to book a consultation?
Yes. A small deposit is requested to secure your appointment, which will be deducted from the final treatment cost.
12. Are there any hidden fees or consultation charges?
No. The online assessment is free, and all pricing is communicated transparently before booking.
13. I have had aesthetic treatments elsewhere - can I continue treatment at Skinity?
Yes. Your previous treatment history will be carefully reviewed during your assessment. In some cases, a waiting period or treatment adjustment may be recommended to ensure safety and optimal results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most common aesthetic treatments?
The most popular treatments include Botox for wrinkle reduction, dermal fillers for volume restoration, skinboosters for hydration and skin quality improvement, and thread lifts for facial lifting and contouring.
How long do the results last?
Results vary by treatment: Botox typically lasts 3-6 months, dermal fillers 6-18 months, skinboosters 6-12 months, and thread lifts 1-2 years. Individual results may vary based on skin type, lifestyle, and treatment area.
Is there any downtime after treatments?
Most treatments have minimal downtime. You may experience slight redness, swelling, or bruising that typically resolves within 24-48 hours. You can usually return to normal activities immediately after treatment.
Are the treatments painful?
Discomfort is minimal and temporary. We use topical anesthetics and fine needles to ensure your comfort. Most clients describe the sensation as a slight pinch or pressure.
How do I know which treatment is right for me?
During your consultation, we'll assess your skin concerns, goals, and medical history to recommend the most suitable treatment plan. We always prioritize natural-looking results and your safety.
What should I do before my assessment?
Avoid blood-thinning medications (with doctor's approval), alcohol 24 hours before, and arrive with clean skin. We'll provide specific pre-treatment instructions during your consultation.
How often should I schedule maintenance treatments?
Maintenance schedules vary by treatment and individual needs. We'll create a personalized plan during your consultation to help maintain optimal results.
Are the treatments safe?
Yes, all treatments are FDA-approved and performed by qualified professionals using sterile techniques and high-quality products. We prioritize your safety and natural-looking results.

skin boosters – deep hydration & skin revitalisation

Skin boosters are advanced injectable treatments designed to deeply hydrate, improve skin quality, and restore a youthful glow — from the inside out. Unlike traditional fillers, skin boosters don't add volume. Instead, they work at a cellular level to nourish and revitalise the skin over time.

hyaluronic acid-based boosters

These boosters use high-quality hyaluronic acid to improve skin elasticity, hydration, and smoothness. Ideal for treating fine lines, crepey texture, and dull skin.

  • Profhilo – boosts collagen and elastin with a bio-remodelling effect
  • Seventy Hyal 200 – intense hydration and skin glow
  • Juvéderm Hydrate & Restylane Vital – targeted hydration and skin texture improvement

polynucleotide-based boosters

Polynucleotides work by stimulating tissue regeneration and repairing damaged skin.

  • Lumi Eyes – specifically designed for the delicate under-eye area, brightens, hydrates, and reduces dark circles

exosome-based boosters

Exosomes are cutting-edge, cell-signaling molecules that promote skin regeneration, improve elasticity, and enhance overall skin tone. These are perfect for those seeking high-performance, scientifically advanced rejuvenation.

quick recovery

Skin boosters involve minimal downtime. Mild redness or swelling may occur but usually settles within 24–48 hours. You can return to your normal activities almost immediately.

indicated for:

  • Dehydrated or dull skin
  • Fine lines and crepey texture
  • Loss of elasticity or firmness
  • Tired-looking under-eyes
  • Skin in need of deep hydration and renewal
  • Clients wanting a subtle, natural glow without added volume

Skinboosters

Skinboosters are treatments often described as injectable "serums," using high-quality substances to deliver deep hydration and revitalize your skin from within. Many skinboosters contain hyaluronic acid, which improves skin texture, firmness, and luminosity by stimulating natural collagen production, resulting in a healthy, radiant glow.

Additionally, there are skinboosters based on polynucleotides, such as Lumieyes, which work to regenerate and repair the skin, especially in the delicate eye area, helping reduce dark circles, fine lines, and signs of fatigue.

They are suitable for all ages: some products are ideal for mature skin, focusing on firmness and reducing deeper wrinkles, while others are perfect for those wanting to treat the first signs of aging, such as loss of hydration and fine lines. We also offer specific options for acne-prone skin, helping to improve texture and reduce imperfections.

We work with leading brands such as Lumipro, Profhilo, Restylane, and Seventy Hyal 2000 (hyaluronic acid) and Lumieyes (polynucleotides), ensuring incredible and personalized results for each client.

Quick Recovery

Skinboosters require minimal recovery time. Some redness or slight swelling may occur in the treated area, but these effects usually disappear within 24 to 48 hours. Most people resume their daily activities immediately after treatment.

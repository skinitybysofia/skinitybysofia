Aqualyx - Fat Dissolving Treatment

What is Aqualyx?

Aqualyx is an advanced injectable treatment designed to permanently reduce localized fat in areas like the double chin, jawline, and other stubborn pockets. Using deoxycholic acid, a naturally occurring molecule in the body, it safely dissolves fat cells, helping you achieve refined contours and a sculpted silhouette without surgery.

Ideal for clients seeking subtle yet effective body and facial shaping, Aqualyx delivers gradual, natural-looking results, often visible after a few sessions. Each treatment takes only 30 minutes, with minimal downtime.

Clinically Proven and Trusted: Aqualyx is backed by robust scientific studies and trusted by aesthetic experts worldwide. The Aesthetic Surgery Journal (2021) confirmed safety and efficacy, showing significant improvements in facial and body contour with minimal side effects. The Journal of Cosmetic Dermatology (2015) demonstrated effective reduction of double chin fat, with visible results after a few sessions. These studies confirm that Aqualyx is a safe, effective, and reliable medical solution for non-surgical fat contouring.

fat dissolving injections – targeted contouring without surgery

Fat dissolving injections are non-surgical treatments designed to break down stubborn fat deposits in specific areas of the body. These treatments work by liquefying fat cells, which are then naturally eliminated by the body over time. Ideal for sculpting and refining, they offer a non-invasive alternative to liposuction with minimal downtime.

Fat dissolving treatments are safe, effective, and suitable for both men and women seeking subtle but noticeable contouring results.

lemon bottle

A next-generation fat dissolving treatment combining plant-based ingredients, amino acids, and vitamins for enhanced lipolytic action.

  • How it works: Lemon Bottle accelerates fat breakdown through a synergistic blend of natural compounds. It also improves lymphatic drainage and circulation.
  • Benefits: Faster visible results, reduced swelling compared to traditional formulations, and added skin tightening and detoxifying effects.
  • Popular treatment areas: Double chin, jawline, abdomen, thighs, arms, and bra bulge.
  • Ideal for: Clients looking for a gentler, faster-acting fat-dissolving option with added skin benefits.

aqualyx

Aqualyx is a biocompatible, injectable fat-reduction treatment specifically formulated to target and eliminate stubborn pockets of fat.

  • How it works: Aqualyx contains deoxycholic acid, a naturally occurring bile acid that dissolves fat cell membranes. Once broken down, the fat is flushed out by the lymphatic system.
  • Benefits: Precision contouring of resistant fat areas, long-lasting results with a course of treatments.
  • Popular treatment areas: Chin, flanks, upper arms, stomach, thighs, and back rolls.
  • Ideal for: Clients with stubborn, exercise-resistant fat deposits who want defined shaping without surgery.

quick recovery

Fat dissolving injections involve little to no downtime. Some swelling, redness, or tenderness is common in the treated area and typically resolves within a few days. Light massage and increased hydration are often recommended to support drainage.

indicated for:

  • Stubborn fat under the chin (double chin)
  • Localized fat pockets on body areas resistant to diet/exercise
  • Bra bulge, love handles, inner thighs, and lower abdomen
  • Clients seeking contouring without invasive procedures
  • Those aiming for gradual, natural-looking fat reduction

Define your features and reveal a slimmer silhouette — naturally. With targeted fat-dissolving injections like Lemon Bottle and Aqualyx, you can achieve a more contoured, confident you — without surgery.

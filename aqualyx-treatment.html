Aqualyx – Fat Dissolving Treatment

Aqualyx is a non-surgical treatment developed for the safe and effective elimination of small areas of localized fat that resist diet and exercise. It is especially popular for areas such as the double chin, arms, flanks, abdomen, and thighs.

The active substance in Aqualyx is deoxycholic acid, a natural compound produced by our body that helps digest and eliminate fat. When injected directly into fat cells (adipose tissue), deoxycholic acid destroys the membrane of these cells, releasing the fat, which is then naturally eliminated through the lymphatic system and the body's metabolism.

Proven Safety and Effectiveness

Aqualyx is appropriate and approved for medical use, with strict protocols to ensure patient safety.

Unlike invasive treatments, it does not require general anesthesia, incisions, or long recovery times.

The most common side effects are temporary and mild, such as redness, swelling, or local sensitivity, usually disappearing within a few days.

Scientific Studies and Approval

Numerous scientific studies validate the effectiveness of deoxycholic acid for localized fat reduction:

  • A review study published in the Aesthetic Surgery Journal in 2021 confirmed the safety and efficacy of deoxycholic acid for localized fat reduction, highlighting significant improvements in facial and body contour definition with minimal side effects.
  • A study published in the Journal of Cosmetic Dermatology (2015) demonstrated that deoxycholic acid is effective for reducing double chin fat, with visible results after a few sessions and minimal side effects.

These data confirm that Aqualyx is a valid, safe, and effective medical solution for those seeking to contour localized fat without surgery.

Why Choose Aqualyx?

  • It is a minimally invasive treatment performed in-office, with quick and virtually painless sessions.
  • Ideal for those who want to avoid surgery and the risks associated with anesthesia or long recovery times.
  • Provides natural and gradual results without altering body shape but improving contour.
  • Suitable for various areas, especially zones difficult to treat with diet and exercise.
